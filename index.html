Tsukiyomi ツキヨミ | Heaven Omakase Restaurant

Concept

Tsukiyomi ツキヨミ, representing the moon god in Japanese mythology, can be associated with a sense of serenity, elegance, and mystique. The word "Heaven" adds an element of luxury and divine dining. "Omakase" implies a high-quality, chef-driven experience where the chef curates a special meal for each customer.
